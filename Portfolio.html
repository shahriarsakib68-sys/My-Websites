I'm Shahriar Shakib — a multi-disciplinary visual creator based in Sylhet, Bangladesh. I specialize in video editing, graphic design, and photography, with a sharp eye for detail and a deep passion for visual storytelling.

Whether it's a cinematic edit, a bold brand identity, a clean photograph, or a coded interface — I bring the same level of craft and intention to every single project.

I believe in the power of visuals to communicate what words can't.
